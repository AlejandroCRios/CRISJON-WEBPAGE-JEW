Repairing jewelry

Our experienced team of jewelers are capable of working on polishing, soldering, repairing of missing prongs, broken shanks, bracelets or chains. We also offer ring sizing, diamond settings, casting of silver, gold and platinum and a complete process of custom jewelry.

We invite you to bring us your ideas and we will make them reality with the help of our state of the art machinery and experienced team.

Our Services

Custom designs: Our main job is to manufacture and create custom jewelry through our high-quality machinery, designers, and jewelers. We invite you to let us turn your ideas into reality.

3D Printing: We offer the printed model of your custom piece which will give you the idea of what it is going to look like. Also, it will allow us to get the final product in the metal you have chosen.

Ring Sizing: We are able to stretch or shrink silver, gold and platinum rings to the desired size.

Casting: This is one of our most important process; the molten metal is injected into a plaster’s mold using a centrifuge, that is how we get your piece in metal. We cast sterling silver, gold and platinum.

Stone settings: We set and supply diamonds and gemstones and will review all prong work and tighten or re-tip prongs where necessary.

Jewelry cleaning and polishing: In order to get a high-quality product, we use an ultrasonic cleaner, a steam cleaner and complete polishing equipment capable of making your pieces look radiant.

Rhodium plating: We offer black, white and yellow rhodium plating services on rings, pendants, necklaces, bracelets and earrings, including two tone pieces. This process gives brilliance and style to your jewelry, such a finesse way of finishing up your custom piece.

Laser engraving on metals: We can engrave on all kind of metal pieces: you could add a name, a quote, a picture or just a personalized message which will give a unique extra meaning to your custom product.
