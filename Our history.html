A lifetime making custom jewelry

With consistency, ethic, and responsibility, Crisjon is recognized as a provider of excellent attention to detail to develop bespoke jewelry and to give excellent services for its clients. Crisjon Fine Jewelry was established in 1999, the founders are Wilmar Lopez along with his wife Maria Lopez.

Crisjon Fine Jewelry offers services as wholesale repairing, manufacturing, and customizing jewelry. Since then, the pair has dedicated their time in building a strong team known for their commitment, hard work, and diligent services for the demanding process of creating jewelry.

Wilmar Lopez has been in the jewerly industry for over 30 years, he first started his workshop in Wilmette, Illinois, creating custom designs for several stores and selling his own products as well. After a year Mr.Lopez decided to relocate to the Jewelers Center of Chicago, where he was able to be further reconized for his fine craftsmanship. With the opportunity of relocating into a larger shop he now has the machineries capable of developing high-quailty jewelry, and has became one of the forefront jewelers in the Chicagoland area.
