Manufacturing, repairing and customizing jewelry since 1999.


Crisjon Fine Jewelry was established in 1999, the founders are Wilmar Lopez along with his wife Maria Lopez. Crisjon Fine Jewelry offers services as wholesale repairing, manufacturing, and customizing jewelry. Since then, the pair has dedicated their time in building a strong team known for their commitment, hard work, and diligent services for the demanding process of creating jewelry.

We like to believe that small details make a big difference

What do we do?

At Crisjon fine jewelry we provide repairing, ring sizing, jewelry cleaning, stones setting, rhodium plating, engraving on metals, and a complete custom design. Feel free to contact us and we will be more than happy to make your idea(s) into reality.
